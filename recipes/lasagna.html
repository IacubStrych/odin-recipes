Italian lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and cheese. It is baked until golden and bubbly, making it a comforting and hearty meal perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

What will you need:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese

Cooking steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef with chopped onions and garlic.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the meat mixture and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
